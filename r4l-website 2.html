Our Mission

More Than Removal.
It's a Movement.

Louis Lee founded R4L with one conviction: every discarded item is an opportunity. Too much ends up in landfills that could be repurposed, donated, or recycled.

We sort with intention on every job. Clothing goes to community partners. Functional furniture is donated. Everything else goes through certified processors.
