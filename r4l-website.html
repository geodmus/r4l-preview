Our Mission

More Than Junk Removal. It's a Movement.

Louis Lee founded Recycle 4 Life Global with a single conviction: every item discarded is an opportunity missed. Too much ends up in landfills that could be repurposed, donated, or recycled.

From Day 1, R4L has operated on the Triple-R philosophy — Repurpose, Recycle, Reuse — applied to every load we haul. When we pick up your items, we sort with intention. Wearable clothing goes to community partners. Functional furniture is donated. Everything else is recycled through certified processors.

We are not just a hauling company. We are a community resource that happens to own trucks.

🤝 Reuse for Community

Got Items That Could Help Someone?
